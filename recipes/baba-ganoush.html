Baba Ganoush

A traditional Middle-Easter appetizer. Served with pita.

Ingredients
  • Eggplants
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Cinnamon
  • Mint
  • Parsley
  • Tahini
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic

  1. Preheat over to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Halve egglplants.
  3. Dice garlic.
  4. Use brush to cover flat side of eggplant with olive oil.
  5. Season flat side with smoked paprika.
  6. Place flat side of eggplant on grill until caramelized. (Approx 35-40 mins)
  7. Remove eggplants from grill and using a fork scoop contents of eggplant into a bowl.
  8. Mix contents of eggplant with tahini and diced garlic.
  9. Add salt, olive oil, cinnamon and lemon juice.
  10. Garnish with mint and parsley.
