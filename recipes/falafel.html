Falafel

Description: A traditional Middle-Eastern Snack

Ingredients
  • Dried Chickpeas
  • Parsley
  • Cilantro
  • Dill
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Canola Oil?

  1. Soak chikpeas for 24 hours. Add baking soda to soften them more quickly. Drain very well.
  2. Add chickpeas, parsley, cilantro, dill, onions, and garlic to food processor and pulse until mixture is finely ground.
  3. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Once removed from refrigeration, stir in baking powder and toasted sesame seeds. Scoop into golf-ball sized balls and form into balls.
  5. Heat oil on medium-high until soft bubbling. (About 375 Degrees F). Drop balls into oil using slotted spoon and fry for 3-5 minutes until medium brown on the outside.
  6. Serve in pita or with salad on side.
