Shakshuka

Description: A traditional Middle-Eastern dish.

Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Bell Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Basil Leaves
  • Thyme
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Powder
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Water
  • Sugar

  1. Cube tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers.
  2. Dice garlic.
  3. Pour 1 tsp of oil into large bowl and place contents above in bowl.
  4. Mix with seasoning excluding sugar.
  5. Heat pan with oil.
  6. Pour contents of bowl in pan.
  7. Once pan contents are boiling, pour 1 tsp of sugar in contents.
  8. Using a spoon, create "dunes" in pan of contents and break eggs into said open pockets of space.
  9. Leave cooking for 20 minutes.
  10. Enjoy life.
